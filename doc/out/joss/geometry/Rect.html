joss/geometry/Rect

Describes a rectangle and provides methods for manipulating it. Inspired by the Qt library's QRect class.

Rect moveBottom(Number y)

Rect moveTo(Point p)

Moves the rectangle, leaving the top-left corner at the given position (x, y). The rectangle's size is unchanged.
